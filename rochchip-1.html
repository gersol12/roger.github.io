Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is rock chip repair?

Rock chip repair is a process that restores the integrity of your vehicle's windshield after it has been damaged by a rock or debris.

How long does the repair take?

The repair process typically takes around 30 minutes, depending on the extent of the damage.

Will my insurance cover the repair?

Many insurance policies cover rock chip repairs, but it is best to check with your provider for details.

Do you provide mobile services?

Yes, we offer mobile rock chip repair services, meaning we can come to your location for repairs.
